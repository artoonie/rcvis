Ranked Choice Voting Election Results\n\n\nCity of Eastpointe, Macomb County, MI
In this ranked choice voting election, there were 5 rounds, after which Harvey Curley and Larry Edwards were elected. Here's what happened in each round. 
In the first round, Larry Edwards received the most votes. 
In the second round, Write-In had the fewest votes and was eliminated. Harvey Curley received the most votes. 
In the third round, Harvey Curley had the most votes and was elected. Harvey Curley had more than enough votes to win, so to ensure no vote is wasted, 10 surplus votes were redistributed to other candidates. 
In the fourth round, Sarah Lucido had the fewest votes and was eliminated. Larry Edwards received the most votes. 
In the fifth round, Larry Edwards had the most votes and was elected. Larry Edwards had more than enough votes to win, so to ensure no vote is wasted, about 50 surplus votes were redistributed to other candidates.
