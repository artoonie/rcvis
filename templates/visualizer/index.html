    What is Ranked Choice Voting?

    With Ranked Choice Voting, you can rank your candidates in your order of preference. If your top pick loses with the fewest votes, your vote isn't wasted: that candidate is eliminated and your vote is transferred to your next choice.

    Learn more on our medium post: An Illustrated Guide to Ranked Choice Voting.
